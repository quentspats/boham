BOHAM Praia · Website overview

Your website. Fully yours — and built to grow.

What you're looking at is a working design preview of the BOHAM Praia website. It's real, live, and on the screen — not a slideshow. This page explains what you'll be able to edit yourself, and the custom features we can add as BOHAM grows from a beach club into a kite club and boutique destination.

Edit it yourself

No code. A simple login where you change everything yourself.

We connect the site to a content manager (CMS) — a private, easy dashboard. You log in, click on what you want to change, type or upload, and save. No developer needed for day-to-day updates. If you can use Instagram, you can use this.

✎

Text & headlines

Rewrite any title, paragraph, button or tagline across the whole site, whenever you want.

◳

Photos & gallery

Swap the hero image, replace any photo, and add or reorder the Instagram-style gallery in seconds.

☷

Menu, hours & info

Update the food & drinks, opening hours, address and contact details directly — they show instantly.

✦

Sections & events

Add new sections, announce events or DJ nights, and publish or hide blocks as the season changes.

Custom features we can add

The design is only the surface. Below are integrations we can build on top — added in phases, whenever you're ready. These are the things that actually drive bookings, revenue and community.

Bookings & reservations

One-tap WhatsApp booking, plus optional online table & experience reservations with confirmations.

Kite Club & memberships

Newsletter sign-up, guest list capture, event invites and automated follow-ups to bring people back.

Private investor area

A password-protected section for the boutique resort vision — renders, plans and materials for partners only.
