The BOHAM experience

The most iconic beach club in Norte de Brazil — one place for the whole day by the sea.

Arrive in the morning, kite or swim at midday, lunch with your feet in the sand, then stay for cocktails and music as the sun goes down. Part kite point, part beach club, part restaurant, part sunset bar.

Drop your kite. Grab a beer. Stay for the sunset.

A day at BOHAM

From morning calm to sunset glow.

However you start, the sea sets the rhythm. Here's how a day on the sand usually unfolds.

Morning

Slow start

Arrive at the beach, coffee in hand, the wind still soft. Set up your kite gear and ease into the day.

Midday

Kite & swim

The wind fills in. Catch a session on flat water, cool off in the pool, then lunch with your feet in the sand.

Afternoon

Lounge & meet

Drinks by the pool, bean bags, good company and the easy social buzz that makes BOHAM what it is.

Sunset

Music & cocktails

DJ sets and live sessions, a BOHAM Beach in hand, and the sky turning every shade of orange.

Restaurant & bar

Beach cuisine, cocktails & long sunset tables.

Cozinha de praia made for long days by the sea — relaxed beach lunches, açaí bowls, fresh salads, burgers and fish, served right where the wind meets the water.

When the afternoon turns golden, the bar takes over: caipirinhas, signature cocktails and cold drinks for big group tables that stretch into the sunset.

Kite

Kite today. Club tomorrow.

BOHAM sits right on one of Brazil's best kite spots — steady wind, flat water, part of the Rota das Emoções between Jericoacoara and the Parnaíba Delta. Today it's a kite point and social base; the full kite club is the next chapter.

The destination

Barra Grande, Piauí.

A peaceful fishing village on the Rota das Emoções — known for wind, kitesurfing, natural beauty, mangroves and an easygoing rhythm. A place to disconnect, between Jericoacoara, the Parnaíba Delta and the Lençóis Maranhenses.

BOHAM was created to celebrate that lifestyle, from morning to sunset.
